# Strategic Risks

## Market Framing Risk

Many organisations still treat frontline training as part of traditional LMS-based learning.

If buyers continue to rely on large enterprise learning systems or prioritise office-worker training needs, they may fail to recognise the need for a dedicated frontline operational enablement platform.

This could slow adoption even if eduMe provides superior functionality.

---

## Compliance Anxiety Unknown

We believe admins value the ability to prove compliance and training completion.

However we do not yet know what specifically creates the most anxiety during audits or compliance reviews.

Possible areas:

* proof training was assigned
* proof workers completed it
* proof workers understood it
* proof managers verified it
* proof processes were followed

Next step
Interview customers who have recently gone through audits or insurance reviews to understand what evidence was required and what was hardest to produce.
