# Strategic Bets

## Bet 1 — Operational Compliance Platform

Hypothesis
If eduMe becomes the system organisations rely on to prove that training, audits and processes have been delivered and followed, we become a need-to-have platform rather than a discretionary training tool.

Implications for Product
Focus on:

* evidence of training delivery
* audit tracking
* process verification
* compliance reporting
* defensible record keeping

Outcome
eduMe becomes the system organisations rely on to demonstrate operational due diligence.

---

## Bet 2 — Operational Knowledge Rollouts

Hypothesis
If eduMe supports operational knowledge distribution (not just training), we become the platform organisations use to run operational rollouts across frontline teams.

Examples

* onboarding
* safety procedures
* SOP distribution
* operational updates
* system rollouts
* compliance training

Outcome
eduMe becomes the system that runs frontline operational knowledge.

---

## Bet 3 — AI-Driven Operational Enablement

Hypothesis
Admins will strongly prefer systems that automate operational enablement.

AI can reduce workload by:

* generating training from SOPs or policies
* identifying knowledge gaps
* recommending rollout campaigns
* targeting the correct workers
* automating reminders
* generating compliance reports

Outcome
eduMe becomes the lowest-effort system for running frontline enablement.
