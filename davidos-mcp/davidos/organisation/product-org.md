# Product Organisation

## Product Organisation Vision

Build a product organisation that learns faster than the market changes.

Key characteristics:

### Fast experimentation

Teams can quickly test ideas, prototype new workflows and validate concepts before committing large development investment.

Goal
Reduce time from idea → learning.

---

### Deep customer research

The team builds real understanding of customer operational workflows and frontline environments.

Research should answer strategic questions rather than simply validating features.

---

### Timely insight

Insights arrive when they can still influence product decisions.

Research and experimentation should occur continuously rather than after roadmap commitments are made.

---

## 6-Month Organisational Priority

Accelerate experimentation across the product organisation.

Why this matters
AI and new product paradigms are evolving quickly. The organisation must be able to test ideas, prototype new workflows and learn from real customer behaviour much faster.

Desired outcomes:

* teams regularly prototype and test ideas
* AI workflows are explored through experiments
* discovery cycles are shorter
* product decisions are informed by real learning
